* When a user asks for growth of company they are interested in year by year revenue.
Always translate business terms:
COUNT(sale_id) as "Total sales"
SUM(product_quantity_sold * Price) → "Total Revenue"
COUNT(claim_id)/COUNT(sale_id) → "Total Claim rate %"

* When a user asks for growth of a particular year they are interested in month by month revenue in that year.

* month name in sale_month 
1 = 'January'
2 = 'February'
3 = 'March'
4 = 'April'
5 = 'May'
6 = 'June'
7 = 'July'
8 = 'August'
9 = 'September'
10 = 'October'
11 = 'November'
12 = 'December'

* Quarter in sale_month
1,2,3 = 'First Quarter' or 'Q1'
4,5,6 = 'Second Quarter' or 'Q2'
7,8,9 = 'Third Quarter' or 'Q3'
10,11,12 = 'Last Quarter' or 'Q4'

* For forecasting and predict questions, always show:
1. Historical trend (2023-2024) + 2025 prediction
2. Growth rate assumption
3. Use sales_forecast_2025 view first
4. For any predict using the historical trends use the previous available year as the base year for projection. 

* PII GOVERNANCE RULES:
1. NEVER show raw data for sale_id, email and phone_no from sales table
2. If column tag pii_flag : true detected the response = "You don't have access to PII columns. Masking applied"
3. In any query if column sale_id is detected, never run the select sale_id column query. Show all other columns instead
4. Use the "sales_governed" dataset while creating query with phone_no or email
5. Block queries asking for "customer details", "contact info", "email", "phone no" and "phone number"

* For SUMMARY OR INSIGHTS always provide the below all 8 details:
1. Give the total revenue of the year.
2. What is the difference between revenue of current year and previous year.
3. Based on the revenue the Growth is positive or negative.
4. Which region has the highest and lowest revenue. what was the revenue of the same regions for previous years. 
5. Which product is selling more and least.
6. What is the claim rate of the mentioned year and which product has the more claims.
7. What is the predicted revenue for the next year.
8. Which store sold the most no of products.
